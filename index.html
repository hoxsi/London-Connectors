About London Connector

London Connector was founded to solve a simple problem: networking should be more than just collecting business cards. It should be about creating meaningful, in-person connections that lead to real business outcomes.

We focus on high-quality venues, a curated audience, and an atmosphere that encourages authenticity. Today, we are one of London's most active and trusted business event platforms.
